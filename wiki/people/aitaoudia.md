# Fayçal Aït Aoudia

## Affiliation
**NVIDIA** (Sionna team), based in France. Email: `faitaoudia@nvidia.com`. **One of the three primary NVIDIA-intern target authors** alongside [[hoydis]] and [[cammerer]].

## Why he matters
- **End-to-end learning of OFDM systems** — [[paper-aitaoudia-hoydis-2020-ofdm]] (arxiv:2009.05261) co-author. Showed how to learn pilot patterns + transmitter + receiver jointly.
- **Model-free training of E2E communication systems** (arxiv:1812.05929; IEEE JSAC 2019) — co-author with Hoydis. Fundamental autoencoder-PHY paper.
- **Sionna co-author** — [[paper-sionna-2022]], [[paper-sionna-rt-2023]], [[paper-diff-rt-calibration-2024]]. He's listed on most Sionna-team papers as Hoydis's closest collaborator.
- **Neural receiver line** — [[paper-nrx-cammerer-2023]], [[paper-nrx-wiesmayr-2024]] co-author.

## Topics he works on
- Differentiable signal processing, autoencoder-based PHY, neural receivers, E2E learning, OFDM pilot design.

## How he intersects the roadmap
[[python-ml-wireless]] Phase 2 M4 (autoencoder reproduction), Phase 3 M7 (Sionna NRX modification), and Phase 4 M10 (NRX Wiesmayr + diff-RT calibration) all cite him. Reading and citing him alongside Hoydis is the cold-email signal.
