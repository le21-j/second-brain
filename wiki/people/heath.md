# Robert Heath Jr.

**Affiliation:** Distinguished Professor, North Carolina State University (moved from UT Austin); also Cullen Trust for Higher Education Endowed Chair, UT Austin (emeritus).
**Research:** mmWave MIMO, beam management, vehicular communications, ISAC.

## Why he matters to Jayden

Heath is **Ahmed Alkhateeb's PhD advisor** (UT Austin 2016). The Wi-Lab research program descends directly from Heath's mmWave + situational-awareness work, especially:

- **Wang, Narasimha, Heath 2018** — "MmWave Beam Prediction with Situational Awareness" (SPAWC) — the precursor to all of Wi-Lab's multi-modal beam prediction.
- **Heath + Gonzalez-Prelcic** — *Foundations of Signal Processing for Machine Learning* (upcoming textbook + lecture notes, widely circulated).

## Why to know him

- Understanding his papers clarifies the *intellectual lineage* of Wi-Lab — this helps in cold-email framing.
- Any Wi-Lab / Heath-adjacent paper cites the mmWave MIMO signal-processing canon he defined.
