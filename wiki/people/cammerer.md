# Sebastian Cammerer

## Affiliation
**NVIDIA** (Sionna team), Germany. Email: `scammerer@nvidia.com`. PhD from U. Stuttgart under Stephan ten Brink. **One of the three primary NVIDIA-intern target authors** alongside [[hoydis]] and [[aitaoudia]].

## Why he matters
- **Neural decoder pioneer.** Gruber, Cammerer, Hoydis, ten Brink 2017 — *On Deep Learning–based Channel Decoding* (CISS) — one of the seminal neural-decoder papers.
- **Neural receiver lead.** First author of [[paper-nrx-cammerer-2023]] (NVIDIA neural receiver for 5G NR multi-user MIMO, arxiv:2312.02601). Co-author on [[paper-nrx-wiesmayr-2024]] standard-compliant real-time variant.
- **Sionna co-author** — [[paper-sionna-2022]], [[paper-sionna-rt-2023]], [[paper-diff-rt-calibration-2024]].
- **Stuttgart connection.** PhD pedigree: same lab as ten Brink and Euchner — DICHASUS sounder dataset originates here.
- **Sionna Research Kit 2025** (arxiv:2505.15848) co-author.

## Topics he works on
- Neural decoders, neural receivers, autoencoder-PHY, end-to-end learning, link adaptation.

## How he intersects the roadmap
[[python-ml-wireless]] Phase 3 M7 (Sionna NRX modification — directly his code), Phase 4 M10 (Wiesmayr + diff-RT calibration). The NRX repository (https://github.com/NVlabs/neural_rx) is his — modifying it is the headline NVIDIA-portfolio move.
