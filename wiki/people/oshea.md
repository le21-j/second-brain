# Tim O'Shea

**Affiliation:** Co-founder and CTO, [DeepSig](https://www.deepsig.ai/); formerly GPU RF research; active in AI-RAN Alliance.
**Twitter/X:** `@oshtim` (verified, active — primary professional channel)

## Why he matters

O'Shea is the **de facto popularizer of deep learning for wireless**. His 2017 paper with Hoydis is the field's founding document; his RadioML datasets are the field's ImageNet; his IEEE ICC ML4Wireless workshop is where the community meets.

## Canonical works

- **O'Shea & Hoydis 2017** — "An Introduction to Deep Learning for the Physical Layer," IEEE TCCN (arxiv:1702.00832). The seminal PHY-ML paper. See [[autoencoder-phy]].
- **O'Shea, Corgan, Clancy 2016** — the original RadioML CNN paper.
- **O'Shea, Roy, Clancy 2018** — "Over-the-Air DL Based Radio Signal Classification," IEEE JSTSP (arxiv:1712.04578). RadioML 2018.01A. See [[modulation-classification]].
- DeepSig builds commercial products around RF ML — the company is one of the founding members of the AI-RAN Alliance.

## Public outputs

- **RadioML datasets** (2016.10a, 2018.01A) at https://www.deepsig.ai/datasets/.
- **TorchSig** (https://torchsig.com/) — PyTorch-native RF library O'Shea's company helps drive.
- **IEEE ICC ML for Communications workshop** — he's been a repeat co-chair.
