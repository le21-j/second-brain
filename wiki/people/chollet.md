# François Chollet

**Current:** ARC Prize; independent AI research.
**Previous:** Google Research (creator and lead of **Keras**, 2015).
**Books:** *Deep Learning with Python* (2nd ed., Manning 2021) — see [[deep-learning-with-python-chollet]].

## Why he matters to Jayden

Chollet is the **authoritative TensorFlow/Keras teacher** — he wrote Keras. The roadmap uses his book for Phase 2 M6's TensorFlow crash course (Ch 3, 7, 9), which exists only because Sionna 1.x is still TensorFlow-based and a large body of existing wireless-ML code uses Keras subclassing.

**Chapter 7 is the critical one** — `tf.keras.layers.Layer` subclassing is how every Sionna 1.x block is written. Memorize the pattern.

## Also known for

- The **ARC Challenge** — a benchmark for general reasoning that Chollet argues is much harder than typical ML benchmarks, and therefore a better test of progress toward AGI.
- Public writing on AI that's more cautious about LLM capability claims than is common.
