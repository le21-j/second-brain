# Marc Lichtman

**Affiliation:** Research scientist, University of Maryland (UMD); active in DARPA and DoD wireless research.
**Author of:** [PySDR](https://pysdr.org/) — the free online textbook that the roadmap calls "the single most wireless-relevant free resource anywhere."

## Why he matters to Jayden

See [[pysdr-lichtman]] — chapter 12 (OFDM) is the starting point for the Phase 1 M2 "OFDM from scratch" project; chapter 16 (ML for RF) is the gentle on-ramp to the PHY-ML literature. PySDR is CC-licensed, open-source (https://github.com/777arc/PySDR), and continuously updated.

## What makes PySDR special

- **Python-first.** Every DSP concept is in runnable code.
- **Interactive plots.** Many concepts have embedded explorables where you can drag sliders and see the result.
- **Pluto-SDR integration.** Directly relevant to Jayden's AirComp implementation on 4× Pluto SDRs.
- **One-person maintained.** Which is to say, it's what one person's careful pedagogy produces — high-quality, internally consistent, no committee compromises.
