# Sadjad Alikhani

## Affiliation
**Wireless Intelligence Lab @ ASU**, PhD student under [[alkhateeb]]. Email: `alikhani@asu.edu`. **First author of the entire LWM family** — the highest-cited grad student in the Wi-Lab roadmap pillar.

## Why he matters
- **LWM (2024)** — [[paper-lwm-2024]] (arxiv:2411.08872) — first author. The original Large Wireless Model: BERT-style foundation model for channels.
- **LWM-Spectro (2026)** — [[paper-lwm-spectro-2026]] (arxiv:2601.08780) — second author. Foundation model for I/Q spectrograms with mixture-of-experts.
- **LWM-Temporal (2026)** — [[paper-lwm-temporal-2026]] (arxiv:2603.10024) — first author. Sparse spatio-temporal attention for channel trajectories.
- **Hugging Face hub maintainer** for `wi-lab/lwm`, `wi-lab/lwm-spectro`, `wi-lab/lwm-temporal` checkpoints.

## Topics he works on
- Foundation models for wireless, self-supervised pretraining (masked channel modeling, masked spectrogram modeling), sparse attention, transfer learning across propagation environments.

Phase 4 M12 explicitly cites LWM-Spectro and LWM-Temporal as required reading.
