# Andrew Ng

**Affiliations:** Professor, Stanford (adjunct); founder, DeepLearning.AI and Coursera; formerly Chief Scientist at Baidu; co-founder of Google Brain.

## Why he matters to Jayden

**Primary coursework anchor of the roadmap.** Three Coursera specializations drive Phases 1–2:

- **ML Specialization (2022 rebuild)** — https://www.coursera.org/specializations/machine-learning-introduction. Three courses. Python-based.
- **Deep Learning Specialization** — https://www.coursera.org/specializations/deep-learning. Five courses.
- **MLOps / AI Production** — for career polish.

**Course 3 of the DL Specialization ("Structuring ML Projects") is the underrated one** — it teaches how to actually do ML research: error analysis, train/val/test slicing, avoiding data leakage. The roadmap explicitly calls this out.

## Teaching style to imitate

- Careful, slow narration; every equation written by hand.
- Small, focused assignments that build up to meaningful projects.
- Heavy emphasis on the *workflow* of ML, not just the math.
