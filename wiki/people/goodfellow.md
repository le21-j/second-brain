# Ian Goodfellow

**Current:** research scientist, DeepMind (Google).
**Previous:** Apple (ML director), OpenAI, Google Brain.
**PhD:** Montreal under Yoshua Bengio (2014).

## Why he matters to Jayden

Two canonical contributions:

1. **GANs** — Goodfellow et al. 2014 (arxiv:1406.2661). See [[generative-adversarial-network]]. Single-handedly created the modern adversarial-learning family.
2. **The *Deep Learning* book** — with Yoshua Bengio and Aaron Courville, MIT Press 2016, free at https://www.deeplearningbook.org/. The field's first comprehensive textbook. See [[goodfellow-deep-learning]].

The book is now partially dated (pre-transformer, pre-diffusion) but Part I (math refresher) and Ch 6–10 remain the clearest theoretical exposition of the DL foundations.
