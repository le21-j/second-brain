# Emil Björnson

**Affiliation:** Professor, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm.
**Research:** Massive MIMO, 5G/6G communications, holographic surfaces, wireless information theory.
**Blog:** https://ebjornson.com/blog/
**Twitter/X:** more active on LinkedIn than on X.
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/c/WirelessFuture — **"Wireless Future"** with Erik G. Larsson. The roadmap calls this "required viewing."
**Podcast:** same duo, https://wirelessfuture.podbean.com/ — required listening per the roadmap.
**GitHub:** https://github.com/emilbjornson — every repo pairs with a textbook chapter.

## Why he matters to Jayden

Björnson is a **best-in-class wireless-first teacher**. The roadmap calls out his YouTube and podcast as required content. For a DSP applicant transitioning to wireless ML, his videos are the clearest introductions to massive MIMO, cell-free, channel estimation in MIMO, and why classical wireless already has a lot of answers that ML then builds on.

## What to watch

- **MIMO Dimensioning** playlist.
- **Channel Estimation in Massive MIMO.**
- **Cell-free Massive MIMO.**
- Any podcast episode that covers a paper — he'll give the honest "what's new, what's not, what's overhyped" take.
