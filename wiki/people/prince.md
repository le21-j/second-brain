# Simon Prince

**Author of:** *Understanding Deep Learning* (MIT Press, 2023; free PDF at https://udlbook.github.io/udlbook/).
**Previous:** Borealis AI / RBC; computer-vision researcher; earlier "Computer Vision: Models, Learning, and Inference" (Cambridge, 2012).

## Why he matters to Jayden

**Primary DL textbook of the roadmap.** See [[textbook-prince-understanding-deep-learning]].

The book's signature is **pedagogical figures**. Prince spent years on every illustration — the book's reputation comes from readers saying "I finally got [X concept] from Prince's figure for it." Especially for transformers (Ch 12) and diffusion (Ch 18), where other texts struggle.

## What to use him for

- Read a chapter in Prince → run code in [[textbook-d2l-dive-into-deep-learning]] → attempt a relevant CS231n exercise. That's the roadmap's Phase 1–2 cycle.
- For Phase 3's RL arc: Prince Ch 19 is a solid RL refresher after [[textbook-sutton-barto-rl]].
