# Raschka — Machine Learning with PyTorch and Scikit-Learn

**Status:** stub — paid book; **code free at https://github.com/rasbt/machine-learning-book**. Reference card at `raw/textbook/ml-with-pytorch-scikit-learn.md`.

## TL;DR
**The "I know sklearn, now I want PyTorch" bridge.** Raschka's signature move is clarity — every chapter is a minimal working example. Companion to the standalone refresher [PyTorch in One Hour](https://sebastianraschka.com/teaching/pytorch-1h/).

## Topic coverage
- **Classical ML** in scikit-learn (linear/logistic regression, SVM, decision trees, ensembles).
- Data preprocessing, model evaluation, hyperparameter tuning.
- **Intro to deep learning in PyTorch** (MLPs, CNNs, RNNs, transformers).
- **Generative models** (VAEs, GANs).
- **Graph neural networks** (a tour, not a deep dive).
- **Reinforcement learning** intro.
