## Why it's on the roadmap
Fastest on-ramp from "I know some programming" to "I can write a real Python program." Complements CS50P (which emphasizes testing/debugging) by emphasizing project momentum. Matthes is a former teacher; the book is pedagogically excellent. Listed as the book companion to CS50P in the roadmap.

## Chapters that matter for this roadmap
- Chapters 1–10 (fundamentals, lists, dicts, classes, files)
- Chapter 11 (testing) — pytest introduction
- Skip Parts 2 & 3 (game, web) unless time permits
